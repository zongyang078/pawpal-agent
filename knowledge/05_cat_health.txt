Common cat health symptoms
health
cat
Watch for: hiding more than usual, not eating for over 24 hours, excessive grooming or hair loss, changes in litter box habits, vomiting more than once a week, weight loss, excessive thirst, difficulty breathing, and lethargy. Cats are good at hiding illness, so subtle changes in routine can indicate problems. Annual vet visits are essential; twice yearly for cats over 10 years old.
