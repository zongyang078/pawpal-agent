Hamster care basics
general
hamster
Hamsters are nocturnal and most active in the evening. Provide a cage at least 450 square inches of floor space with deep bedding for burrowing. Feed a commercial hamster food mix supplemented with small amounts of fresh vegetables. Provide an exercise wheel (solid surface, not wire) as they run several miles nightly. Handle gently and regularly for socialization. Clean the cage weekly but leave some used bedding so the hamster recognizes its scent. Hamsters live 2-3 years on average.
