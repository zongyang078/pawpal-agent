Dog exercise requirements
health
dog
Most adult dogs need 30-60 minutes of exercise daily. High-energy breeds (Border Collie, Husky, Lab) may need 1-2 hours. Puppies need shorter, more frequent play sessions (5 min per month of age). Senior dogs still need daily walks but at a gentler pace. Signs of insufficient exercise include destructive behavior, excessive barking, weight gain, and restlessness. Mental stimulation through puzzle toys counts too.
