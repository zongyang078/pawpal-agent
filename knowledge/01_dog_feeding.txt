Dog feeding guidelines
feeding
dog
Adult dogs should be fed twice daily, morning and evening. Puppies under 6 months need 3-4 meals per day. Portion size depends on weight: small dogs (under 20 lbs) need about 1 cup daily, medium dogs (20-60 lbs) need 1.5-2.5 cups, large dogs (60+ lbs) need 2.5-4 cups. Always provide fresh water. Avoid feeding chocolate, grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, xylitol, and cooked bones as these are toxic to dogs.
