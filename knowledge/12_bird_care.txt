Bird care basics
general
bird
Birds need a spacious cage they can spread their wings in. Provide a varied diet: pellets as base (60-70%), plus fresh fruits and vegetables daily. Avoid avocado, chocolate, caffeine, and fruit pits which are toxic to birds. Birds need 10-12 hours of sleep in a quiet, dark environment. Social interaction is crucial; spend at least 1-2 hours daily with your bird. Regular vet checkups with an avian specialist are recommended annually.
