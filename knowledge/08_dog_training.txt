Basic dog training principles
training
dog
Use positive reinforcement: reward desired behaviors with treats, praise, or play. Keep training sessions short (5-10 minutes) and end on a positive note. Be consistent with commands and rules across all family members. Start with basic commands: sit, stay, come, down, leave it. Socialization is critical before 16 weeks of age. Never punish a dog for accidents during house training; redirect and reward correct behavior. Professional training classes are recommended for puppies.
